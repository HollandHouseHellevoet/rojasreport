------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  APPLICATION PROCESS
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Maine's CON laws cover a broad range of healthcare services and facilities,
requiring government approval for new projects, expansions, and major
equipment purchases.

  Securing a CON is a lengthy and costly process, giving incumbent providers
ample opportunity to block new competitors.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  MARKET CONCENTRATION
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  CON laws have fostered a consolidated market dominated by a few large health
systems and insurers, reducing competition and driving up costs.

  MaineHealth holds a commanding market share, particularly in the southern
part of the state.
