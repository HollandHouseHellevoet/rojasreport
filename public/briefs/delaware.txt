------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  MARKET CONCENTRATION
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  The state's proximity to Philadelphia means that some Delaware residents
seek care across state lines, which partially mitigates the domestic
concentration. But for the majority of Delawareans — particularly those in
Kent and Sussex counties — the local market is the only practical option.

  Delaware's CON program is moderate by national standards, but that is
grading on a curve. In a state with three counties and two dominant hospital
systems, even a moderate regulatory barrier locks in the existing market
structure.
