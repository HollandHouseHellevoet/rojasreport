------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  APPLICATION PROCESS
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Oklahoma's multi-decade CON reform—from the late 1980s hospital deregulation
through SB 1388 (2018) and HB 2330 (2024)—has eliminated requirements for
hospitals, ASCs, psychiatric facilities, and most acute care. This was real
progress. But the remaining regulations in long-term care still create
barriers where incumbent nursing homes weaponize the appeals process to block
competitors (as documented in the Wood Manor and Hometown Memory Care cases),
and the larger issue is that CON reform alone does not address the structural
advantages that dominant systems like Saint Francis have accumulated through
340B, tax exemption, and market concentration.
